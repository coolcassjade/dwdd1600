What is esthetics? Esthetics "is the branch of philosophy concerned with the nature of beauty and the nature of taste." The profession of esthetician provides services such as:

  • Facials
  • Hair removal
  • Chemical peels
  • Makeup application

On this webpage, we provide insightful information on esthetics. Whether you are a newbie or a full-blown beauty professional, this site brings all the knowledge on history, tips and tricks, current trends, and much more!
